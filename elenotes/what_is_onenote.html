OneNote is a modern digital notebook application designed to increase the productivity of learning and efficiency of class notes management. Dulwich College Suzhou provides everyone with an Office 365 account, allowing OneNote to be accessed by everyone. It is an appropriate idea to replace paper notebooks with the digital notebook, as OneNote will be able to maximize efficiency when users are in the process of producing personalized notes. 

In school, OneNote is frequently put into usage for student and teachers to interact and cooperate. For example, during biology class, illustrating the leaf’s structure on paper notebooks will not be productive. However, simultaneously labeling and annotating the leaf structure with classmates on a shared document on Onenote would enable students as well as teachers to be more efficient and increase productivity. 
  
OneNote enables the users to produce outstanding notes as it provides the users with ultimate functions. First and foremost, the lesson that is being taught can be recorded which can enhance learning as the student is able to listen to it whenever and wherever. Furthermore,  OneNote offers different tools such as pens, markers, and erasers. With the presence of tools, drawings will be completed with more speed since you can simply undo to correct mistakes. Inserting diagrams into OneNote is not all, the user will also be able to add in math equations. OneNote also consists of numerous more wonderful functions that will be waiting to be discovered like being able to transform written equations into text.
