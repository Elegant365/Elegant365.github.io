How to interact with students and give feedbacks?

As a teacher, it is important to give feedback to your students on their work and progress. OneNote is a platform where you can easily set assignments and course resources to your students. Once your students have completed their work, you can mark it and send feedback. 

For you to access each student’s personal notebook, you will need to select “view student’s notebook”. Now, you will be able to see each student’s notebook.

To check for one specific student’s work, click on their notebook with their name on it. 
By selecting the student’s name in the class notebook, you can also choose which notebook you want to review. But this will only apply when the student has operated numerous notebooks. 

After having a look at your student’s work, you’ll then move on to providing feedback. Move your cursor to the “send feedback” icon on the Class notebook tab. Not only will you be able to give a mark, but you can also include written feedback like ‘what went well’ or ‘even better if’ to help your students improve. Your students will then be able to view the feedback you’ve given when they open their notebook.
