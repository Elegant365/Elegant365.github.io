How to work with illustrations in your notebook?

OneNote consists of a wide variety of features that allow users to personalize their notes visually, from illustrations and shapes to highlighters, stickers, and much more. They are advantageous in numeral real-life scenarios, especially when it comes to academics. 

 
During the process of taking notes, it is crucial for a student to highlight keywords and insert diagrams to enhance their learning. 
 
To achieve that, navigate to the Draw tab and select the Highlighter. In this step, not only will the color of the highlighter can be altered but also the thickness, allowing the user to further exemplify their notes. 

 
Moreover, OneNote also has functions which permit images to be inserted or hand-drawn, which can benefit the visual learners significantly. To insert an image, use the cursor and drag the image into the OneNote notebook. On the other hand, to create an image by hand, navigate to the Draw tab, and select the Pen. Being identical to the highlighter, the color and the thickness of the pen can be adjusted. Inserting images is an excellent and efficient way to create notes and creating an image alone can improve the quality of notes.
 
Notes that are produced in OneNote can be classified by navigating to the tab and find “tags”. This will enhance the student’s learning as it will separate and organize their notes into different sections. It is a great accessory which can be utilized to personal preference in order to upgrade and strengthen notes.
