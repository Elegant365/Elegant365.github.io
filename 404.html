     Ever since the beginning of modern civilization, learning has always taken place in the form of pencil and paper. Students and Teachers exchange thoughts and knowledge via paper and learn from paper books. However, this process of managing paper notes and learning resources can be drastically made simpler and efficient using modern digital notebooks such as OneNote. By the end of this course, you will be able to understand how OneNote operates and ways in which we can take lucid digital notes and manage them efficiently.
